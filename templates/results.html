Design Analysis

Professional observations based on industry research.

Brand consistency is the single biggest driver of logistics brand equity. Research by Nielsen shows brands that maintain consistent visual identity across fleet, digital and print touchpoints achieve 23% higher recognition in target markets.

Fleet livery is your most powerful advertising channel — a single HGV generates an estimated 40,000–60,000 impressions per day on motorway routes. The design you choose will represent MI Logistics on every mile driven.

Colour psychology in logistics: Dark backgrounds project authority and premium positioning. Green signals environmental responsibility. Silver and chrome communicate precision and reliability.
